Validated, Culturally Responsive, and DOH-Aligned
In compliance with the Philippine Mental Health Act (RA 11036), DOH Guidelines, and Data Privacy Act (RA 10173).

Purpose: To screen for potential drug use and addiction vulnerability among adolescents and adults (excluding alcohol). For use in schools, workplaces, clinics, and community settings, under ethical and professional supervision.

Demographic Information

Screening Questions (Based on Your Acivities in the Past 12 Months)

"Drug abuse" refers to:

  • Using prescription or over-the-counter drugs more than directed
  • Using any drug for non-medical reasons (e.g., to feel high, stay awake, cope with stress)
  • This includes: cannabis/marijuana (buntot kuting), shabu (methamphetamine), tranquilizers, barbiturates, LSD, sagad/codeine, inhalants (rugby/tuslob), bath salts, spice, and others.
Note: This tool does NOT include alcohol or tobacco.

Informed Consent & Declaration

  • I understand this is a screening tool, not a diagnosis.
  • My responses will be kept confidential and stored securely (RA 10173).
  • Results may be shared only with authorized professionals (e.g., counselor, psychologist, doctor).
  • I have the right to withdraw at any time.
  • I consent to this assessment for self-awareness and early intervention.
For minors (below 18): Parent/guardian must co-sign.
